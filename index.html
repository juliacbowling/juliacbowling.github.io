Guatamalan and Honduran Migration Skyrocket

In the last decade, deportations tripled to Guatamala, and doubled to Honduras, both in the range of 20,000 to 70,000. Deportations to Mexico dropped almost one-third, from 1.1 million to 400,000. In comparison, deportations to Canda dropped below 1,000 in 2007 and again 2013.
